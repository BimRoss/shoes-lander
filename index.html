01 / Intro
A quarterly wellness program

Scanned. Modeled. Shipped.

Custom orthotic insoles for the feet you already have. We scan with the LiDAR sensor in your iPhone, model the correction with AI, and ship a fresh pair every quarter as your gait changes.

02 / Scan
The phone in your pocket

Your foot, mapped.

Stand. Sweep the phone. Done in under a minute. LiDAR captures a 3D mesh of each foot to 2–3 mm tolerance — averaged across passes, the AI targets 1–2 mm. No casting kits, no plaster, no clinic visit.

03 / Model
Correction, learned

Corrected by your data.

Pronation, supination, bunions, leg length discrepancy — the model reads them off the mesh and proposes a correction trajectory. Weekly comfort feedback tunes the next pair. The longer you wear them, the closer the fit gets.

04 / Ship
Direct from the factory

Quarterly, by mail.

A new pair every three months, milled to the latest model output and sent direct from the factory. The feedback loop is the product — your feet move, the insoles move with them. Slide them into the shoes you already own.

05 / About
The team behind the scan

Wellness first — not a medical device.

Built by a small team in New York and Shenzhen. Phase one is a comfort and wellness program; clinical claims, if and when, follow advisors and outcome data. No FDA marketing. No prescription needed. Just better-fitting insoles, on a cadence.
